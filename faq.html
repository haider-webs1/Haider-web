What services do you offer?

What is your typical project process?

My project process typically includes the following steps:

  1. Initial consultation and requirements gathering
  2. Proposal and contract agreement
  3. Design concept and wireframing
  4. Development and coding
  5. Testing and quality assurance
  6. Client review and feedback
  7. Launch and post-launch support

How do you determine project pricing?

Project pricing is determined based on several factors, including:

  • Project scope and complexity
  • Estimated time and resources required
  • Specific features and functionality needed
  • Any third-party integrations or services

I provide detailed quotes after the initial consultation and requirements gathering phase.
