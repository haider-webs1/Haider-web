Frequently Asked Questions

Project timelines vary depending on complexity, but a typical website project takes 4-8 weeks from start to finish.

Do you offer maintenance services after the project is complete?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance and support services to keep your website up-to-date and running smoothly.

Can you work with existing websites or do you only create new ones?

I can work with both existing websites (redesigns or improvements) and create entirely new websites from scratch.

While I can offer guidance on content strategy, clients are typically responsible for providing the content. I can recommend copywriters if needed.
